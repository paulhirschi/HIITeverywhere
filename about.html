High Intensity Interval Training.
The goal is to exercise at 90% to 100% maximum effort for a period of time, followed by a short rest. Complete 8 to 10 reps before giving yourself a longer rest.

The types of workouts do not change based on the skill level you choose. What does change is the amount of time you workout before you rest.

All these HIIT workouts are bodyweight based, meaning you don't need any weights or any other special equipment. Just yourself and some motivation!

All the workouts are pretty well known, especially if you've done HIIT training before. If you are not familiar with a workout, give it a quick web search.
